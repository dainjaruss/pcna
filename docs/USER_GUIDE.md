## Getting Started

### System Requirements
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- JavaScript enabled
- Stable internet connection

### Accessing the App
Navigate to your organization's Pop Culture News App URL. You'll see the login page if you're not already signed in.

---

## Creating Your Account

### Registration
1. Click **"Register"** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters recommended)
4. Optionally add your display name
5. Click **"Create Account"**

### Logging In
1. Enter your registered email address
2. Enter your password
3. Click **"Sign In"**

Your session will remain active for 24 hours. After that, you'll be automatically signed out for security.

## Personalizing Your Feed

### Initial Setup (Onboarding)
When you first log in, you'll be guided through a personalization process:

1. **Select News Sources**: Choose which pop culture news sources you want to follow
2. **Pick Categories**: Select your favorite topics (Music, Movies, TV, Gaming, etc.)
3. **Favorite Celebrities**: Add celebrities you want to track

### Later Adjustments
You can always change your preferences in **Settings** → **Personalization**.

## Browsing Articles

### Home Feed
Your personalized home feed shows articles based on:
- Your selected sources
- Your preferred categories
- Your favorite celebrities
- Our recommendation algorithm (learns from your ratings)

### Article Cards
Each article card displays:
- **Title**: Click to read the full article
- **Source**: Which news outlet published it
- **Credibility Rating**: Our trust score (1-10)
- **Time**: When it was published
- **Category**: Topic classification
- **Summary**: AI-generated brief overview

### Searching
Use the search bar to find specific articles by:
- Keywords
- Celebrity names
- Topic
- Source name

### Filtering
Filter articles by:
- Category (Movies, Music, TV, Gaming, etc.)
- Source
- Date range
- Credibility rating

## Rating Articles

### Why Rate?
Rating articles helps us:
- Personalize your feed better
- Identify high-quality content
- Improve our recommendations

### How to Rate
1. Read or skim the article
2. Click the star rating (1-5 stars)
3. Your rating is saved automatically

### Rating Guidelines
- ⭐ **1 Star**: Poor quality, inaccurate, or irrelevant
- ⭐⭐ **2 Stars**: Below average, somewhat useful
- ⭐⭐⭐ **3 Stars**: Average, meets expectations
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐ **4 Stars**: Good quality, informative
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ **5 Stars**: Excellent, highly recommended

## Managing Settings

### Accessing Settings
1. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner
2. Select **"Settings"** from the dropdown

### Available Settings

#### Profile Settings
- **Display Name**: How your name appears
- **Email**: Your registered email (cannot be changed)

#### Notification Settings
- **Daily Digest**: Enable/disable daily email summary
- **Digest Time**: When to receive your daily digest

#### Content Preferences
- **Preferred Categories**: Topics you want to see more of
- **Favorite Celebrities**: People to track
- **Hidden Sources**: Sources you don't want to see

#### Data Settings
- **Content Retention**: How long to keep your reading history
- **Export Data**: Download all your data
- **Delete Account**: Permanently remove your account

## Daily Email Digest

### What's Included
Your daily digest contains:
- Top articles from your preferred categories
- News about your favorite celebrities
- Trending topics in pop culture
- AI-generated summary of the day

### Customizing Your Digest
1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Enable **Daily Digest**
3. Set your preferred delivery time
4. Choose digest content preferences

### Unsubscribing
To stop receiving the daily digest:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Disable **Daily Digest**
3. Or click "Unsubscribe" in any email

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in
- Check your email and password are correct
- Try the "Forgot Password" link
- Clear your browser cookies and try again
- Contact support if issues persist

### Articles aren't loading
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Try a different browser
- Clear your browser cache

### My feed doesn't match my preferences
- Allow 24 hours for changes to take effect
- Rate more articles to improve recommendations
- Check your source and category selections in Settings

### I'm not receiving daily emails
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify your email address is correct
- Ensure Daily Digest is enabled in Settings
- Add our email to your contacts/safe senders

### The app is running slowly
- Clear your browser cache
- Disable browser extensions
- Try an incognito/private window
- Check if your browser needs updating
